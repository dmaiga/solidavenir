The Gap We Fill

Current Limitations in West Africa:
Payment Systems

Low banking penetration; Mobile Money dominates - international platforms don't support local payment methods

Trust & Transparency

Initial mistrust; cash-based culture; strong need for visible proof of impact

Impact Verification

Strong demand for tangible, visible results - not just text updates

Economic Model

International fees too expensive for African purchasing power

Our Unique Advantages

Mobile-First & Local Payments

Native Mobile Money integration, designed for African connectivity and payment habits.

Blockchain Transparency

Every transaction immutably recorded on Hedera, with field audits and dual reporting.

Fair & Adaptive Pricing

Lower fees tailored to local purchasing power (2-5% social, 5-10% entrepreneurial projects).

Culturally Adapted

Built with local communities, respecting languages, customs and regulatory requirements.
