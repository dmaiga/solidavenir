Our Origin Story

Solid'Avenir was born from recognizing a fundamental paradox: while West Africa has one of the world's strongest traditions of community solidarity, trust barriers often prevent this natural generosity from reaching its full potential.

We observed countless viable projects struggling to secure funding despite abundant willingness to support local initiatives. The disconnect wasn't about lack of resources, but about lack of trust and transparency in how contributions were managed.
