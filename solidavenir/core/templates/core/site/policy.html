Our governance framework built on radical transparency and trust

Solid'Avenir: A Digital Trust Infrastructure

Solid'Avenir is a digital trust infrastructure that merges entrepreneurial crowdfunding with community solidarity. Our governance is built on radical transparency, guaranteed by Hedera Hashgraph technology. The project policy rests on four fundamental pillars:

1. Verification & Compliance

  • Mandatory audit of all project holders
  • Legal and administrative document verification
  • Technical, financial, and social impact analysis
  • Rigorous due diligence process

2. Transparency & Traceability

  • Immutable data on Hedera Hashgraph
  • Public ledger accessible to all contributors
  • Secure storage of field evidence

3. Fund Distribution

  • Milestone-based fund release
  • Proof validation for each disbursement
  • Independent compliance supervision

4. Data Protection

  • No identifiable personal data on distributed ledger
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Strict compliance with local data protection laws
